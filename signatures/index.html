How to Add Your Signature in Gmail

  1. Open your signature page

    Click "View Signature" on your card above

  2. Select and copy the signature

    Press Cmd+A (Mac) or Ctrl+A (Windows) to select all, then Cmd+C / Ctrl+C to copy

  3. Open Gmail Settings

    Click the gear icon → "See all settings" → scroll to "Signature"

  4. Create a new signature

    Click "Create new", name it "Noodle Seed"

  5. Paste your signature

    Click in the signature editor and press Cmd+V / Ctrl+V to paste

  6. Set as default

    Under "Signature defaults", select your new signature for new emails and replies

  7. Save changes

    Scroll to the bottom and click "Save Changes"
